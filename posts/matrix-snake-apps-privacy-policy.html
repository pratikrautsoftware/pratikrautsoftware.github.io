Matrix Snake App's Privacy Policy


ABOUT MATRIX SNAKE 

Matrix Snake is created by Pratik Raut.

Matrix Snake is the same old original snake game in the form of matrix!

 

It consists of the following options
 

Play - starts the matrix arena along with the snake followed by the food after selecting difficulty.

Difficulty - contains the gameplay difficulty levels viz. Easy, Medium, Hard and Custom.

Controls - contains various ways for controlling the snake like Joystick, Dpad and Swipe along with the positions Left, Center and Right.

Snakes - choose from different snakes amongst all six snakes that are unlocked after reaching a specific score.



Controls

Joystick - controls the snake via. virtual joystick

Dpad - controls the snake via. virtual dpad

Swipe - controls the snake by swiping the screen in the specific direction
 

USAGE OF PERMISSIONS

The Matrix Snake app requires the following permissions

READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE

READ_INTERNAL_STORAGE

WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE

 

for saving and retrieving the scores, controls, arenas and settings on/from local storage respectively for proper control of events and workflow of the game.



DATA HANDLING PROCEDURES, DATA RETENTION AND DATA DELETION  

Matrix Snake does not transmit personal or sensitive information nor does it gather user's data or information.
