Temperature Converter


 


Temperature Converter

Version - 1.4


Description


Temperature Converter is a converter which converts temperature from one scale to other scale, viz.


(C - F, C - K, C - R

F - C, F - K, F - R

K - C, K - F, K - R

R - C, R - F, R - K)


and its vice versa.


What's new in this Temperature Converter?


- Most easy and advanced UI for users to easily get the results at their fingertips.

- All conversions of all scales are available.


Scales -


Celsius (C)

Fahrenheit (F)

Kelvin (K)

Rankine (R)


Conversion -


1. Celsius to other scales

Converts temperature from Celsius scale (C) to other scales like Fahrenheit (F), Kelvin (K), Rankine (R)

2. Fahrenheit to other scales

Converts temperature from Fahrenheit scale (F) to other scales like Celsius (C), Kelvin (K), Rankine (R)

3. Kelvin to other scales

Converts temperature from Kelvin scale (K) to other scales like Celsius (C), Fahrenheit (F), Rankine (R)

4. Rankine to other scales

Converts temperature from Rankine scale (R) to other scales like Celsius (C), Fahrenheit (F), Kelvin (K)


How to use?


1. Enter the temperature in any scale in the fields of scales respectively.

2. The field that you enter in will make the whole row (scale elements) black from grey indicating that you have entered this scale for avoiding any confusions.

3. Conversions will be displayed in the fields itself.

4. To reset/clear all the values at once, tap on the (refresh) icon which is located at the left of (three dots menu) which is located at the upper right corner.

5. To exit Temperature Converter, either tap on (three dots menu) and select 'Exit' or simply press the back button to exit after confirmation.
