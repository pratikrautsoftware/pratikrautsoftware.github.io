Battery Alerter


 

 

Battery Alerter

Version - 1.0

 

Battery Alerter alerts by playing sounds at minimum and maximum battery percentages, thus saving the battery life and preventing the opened or running programs from being closed or shut down directly.


Features


- Has setup for two separate percentage levels viz. minimum and maximum along with different sounds for each.

- Shows detailed as well as minimal battery information.

- Automatic and manual switching of alarm for safety purposes.

- User friendly and responsive UI.

FAQ


1. Why doesn't the 'Detailed Battery Information' get updated in realtime when the dialog box is kept open?

Answer

It is cause of the staticness or staticity of the view model of the message or dialog box that it only updates the contents after manually invoking it the next time.


2. How to change the default or set custom alarm or beep sounds?

Answer

Go to the root directory of Battery Alerter i.e where the 'BatteryAlerter.exe' exists and replace the 'beep_1.wav' for low battery percentage and 'beep_2.wav' for high battery percentage with that of the custom ones.
