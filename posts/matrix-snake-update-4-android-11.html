What's new?


In Update 4 (Android 11)


- Added support for Android 11


- Had to manipulate the directory from Android/data to Android/ due to storage restrictions which were introduced in later android versions. (Note: This change only applies for devices with Android 11 or higher versions)
 

- Bug fixes and improvements


Full Change log

 


Initial Release (Play Store)

-Added various controls 

-External controls support


In Update 1 (Play Store)

- Added center position for Joystick and Dpad

In Update 2 (Play Store)

- Added 5 different snakes viz. Red Python, Aqua Serpent, Green Anaconda, Corn Snake, Amblyodipsas

- Added difficulty levels viz. Easy, Normal, Hard and Custom (Note: The 'Hard' difficulty level works in portrait mode only on some devices)
